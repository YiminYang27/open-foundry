# Gold Price Forecast: 2026-2028

Produce a concrete, probability-calibrated gold price forecast covering
three horizons: near-term (Q2-Q4 2026), medium-term (2027), and
longer-term (2028). All claims must be grounded in searched, verified
data. No numbers from memory.

## Key factors to evaluate

### Geopolitical
- Middle East conflict trajectory and Strait of Hormuz disruption status
- Red Sea / Suez Canal shipping disruption (Houthi attacks) and rerouting costs
- US-China tensions, Taiwan Strait risk premium, and Xi-Trump summit outcomes
- Sanctions regime evolution and its effect on gold trade flows
- Ceasefire / de-escalation probability curves (Polymarket, betting markets)

### Macroeconomic
- Fed rate path: current rate, dot plot, market pricing vs Fed guidance
- Inflation trajectory: CPI, core PCE, PPI -- current readings and projected
  pass-through from oil shock
- US labor market: NFP trends, unemployment rate, wage growth
- Global growth outlook: US, EU, China divergence and recession probability
- USD strength (DXY) trajectory and its inverse relationship with gold
- Oil prices and commodity complex linkage to gold

### Gold-specific fundamentals
- Central bank buying trends (PBOC, RBI, emerging market CBs) -- tonnage AND
  dollar value (distinguish mechanical deceleration from intent change)
- ETF flows (GLD, IAU) and the East-West divergence pattern
- Physical demand: China and India jewelry/retail/investment
- Mine supply constraints and all-in sustaining costs (AISC)
- Real yield (TIPS spread) relationship -- structural premium quantification
- CFTC positioning, speculative crowding, and liquidation risk

### Technical structure
- Key support and resistance levels from current price action
- Trend structure: is the bull market intact or has it broken?
- Volume profile and liquidity gaps that define move magnitude
- Moving average confluences (50-day, 200-day) as systematic trigger levels
- Options market: GVZ implied vol, skew, and notable positioning

### Probability calibration
- Are scenario probabilities consistent with options-implied distributions?
- Correlation structure: gold-dollar, gold-real-yields, gold-oil -- current
  regime vs historical norms
- Scenario independence: which scenarios are correlated and how does that
  affect joint probabilities?
- Confidence intervals: all forecast ranges must specify what CI they represent
- Tail risk: 5th and 95th percentile outcomes beyond named scenarios

### Institutional views
- Major investment bank gold targets (Goldman Sachs, JPMorgan, Morgan Stanley,
  Bank of America, Deutsche Bank)
- Post-war revision status: have banks updated their pre-war targets?
- LBMA analyst survey consensus and dispersion
- Historical forecast accuracy of current consensus

## Deliverable

Produce a structured gold price forecast document that includes:

1. **Market snapshot**: current price, key data points, all searched and dated
2. **Scenario framework** with probability-calibrated scenarios for each horizon:
   - Q2-Q4 2026 (near-term)
   - Full year 2027 (medium-term)
   - Full year 2028 (longer-term)
   Each scenario must have: trigger conditions, price range with explicit CI,
   probability calibrated against market-implied data
3. **Technical structure**: key levels, trend status, where the chart agrees
   or disagrees with fundamental scenarios
4. **Risk assessment**: correlation structure, tail risks, margin cascade
   mechanics, liquidity risk
5. **Key risk events calendar**: dates, events, expected gold impact
6. **Monitoring indicators**: tiered by frequency (daily/weekly/monthly),
   with bullish and bearish signal definitions
7. **Unresolved items**: claims that were not challenged or verified
